Engineered Hardwood EPD & HPD, Explained What it means • How it's reported • Why it matters

EPDs and HPDs are disclosure documents for environmental impacts and material health. Reference-only: no product recommendations.

Quick answer

EPDs report lifecycle impacts; HPDs disclose ingredients and potential hazards.

What it is How it's reported Why it matters FAQ

What it is

EPDs summarize lifecycle assessment data; HPDs list ingredients and hazard screening.

How it's reported

Specs may require product-specific or industry-wide EPDs/HPDs with current validity.

Why it matters

These documents support sustainability goals but are not performance ratings.

FAQ

Does an EPD certify sustainability? ⌄

No. It discloses impacts without rating them.

Are HPDs required for every project? ⌄

Only if a project’s sustainability criteria specify them.

Do disclosures affect durability? ⌄

No. They are transparency documents, not performance specs.
