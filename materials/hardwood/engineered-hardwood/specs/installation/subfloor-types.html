Quick answer

Common subfloors include plywood/OSB, concrete, and existing floors with underlayment.

What it is How it's reported Why it matters FAQ

What it is

Each subfloor type has flatness, moisture, and preparation requirements.

How it's reported

Specs list acceptable subfloors and required prep steps.

Why it matters

Using an incompatible subfloor can cause installation failures or warranty issues.

FAQ

Can engineered hardwood go over concrete? ⌄

Yes, with proper moisture control and adhesive/underlayment.

Is OSB acceptable? ⌄

Often yes, but check fastening and flatness requirements.

Can you install over tile? ⌄

Sometimes, if the tile is stable and the system allows floating installation.
