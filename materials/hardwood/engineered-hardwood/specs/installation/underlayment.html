Engineered Hardwood Underlayment, Explained What it means • How it's reported • Why it matters

Underlayment provides cushioning, sound reduction, and sometimes moisture protection. Reference-only: no product recommendations.

Quick answer

Underlayment selection depends on installation method and subfloor type.

What it is How it's reported Why it matters FAQ

What it is

Materials include foam, cork, rubber, or specialty membranes.

How it's reported

Specs list approved underlayments and required ratings (like sound or moisture resistance).

Why it matters

Using the wrong underlayment can affect performance and warranty coverage.

FAQ

Is underlayment required for glue-down? ⌄

Typically no, but moisture membranes may be required.

Does underlayment improve sound? ⌄

Yes, especially in multi-family or upper-level installations.

Can I use any underlayment? ⌄

No. Use the manufacturer-approved underlayment for the product.
