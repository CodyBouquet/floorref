What it is How it's reported Why it matters FAQ

What it is

Coverage often relates to finish wear-through, not scratches or dents.

How it's reported

Warranty documents list term length, maintenance requirements, and exclusions.

Why it matters

Understanding finish warranty terms helps set realistic expectations for wear.

FAQ

Does finish warranty cover scratches? ⌄

Typically no. It covers finish wear-through under normal use.

Do rugs affect coverage? ⌄

Some warranties require protective pads or prohibit certain rug backings.

Is finish warranty prorated? ⌄

Many are prorated after a certain period.
