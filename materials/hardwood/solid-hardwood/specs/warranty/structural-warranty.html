Solid Hardwood Structural Warranty, Explained What it means • How it's reported • Why it matters

Structural warranties cover defects in manufacturing or core integrity under normal use. Reference-only: no product recommendations.

Quick answer

They address issues like delamination or core separation, not surface wear.

What it is How it's reported Why it matters FAQ

What it is

Coverage is typically tied to proper installation and environmental control.

How it's reported

Warranty documents list coverage length and exclusions.

Why it matters

Structural warranties help set expectations about manufacturing quality rather than surface appearance.

FAQ

Does structural warranty cover gapping? ⌄

Usually not if caused by humidity or installation issues.

Is structural warranty transferable? ⌄

It depends on the manufacturer’s policy.

What voids structural coverage? ⌄

Improper installation or moisture exposure often void coverage.
