What it is

They may cover subfloor moisture limits or specific moisture-related defects.

How it's reported

Warranty documents specify testing methods, limits, and exclusions.

Why it matters

Understanding moisture warranty terms is key for concrete or below-grade installs.

FAQ

Does moisture warranty cover flooding? ⌄

Typically no. Flooding and standing water are usually excluded.

Are moisture warranties only for glue-down? ⌄

Not always, but requirements vary by installation method.

Do moisture warranties require documentation? ⌄

Often yes. Testing records may be required for claims.
