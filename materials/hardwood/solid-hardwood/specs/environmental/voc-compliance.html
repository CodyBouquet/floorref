Solid Hardwood VOC Compliance, Explained What it means • How it's reported • Why it matters

VOC compliance indicates the product meets emissions limits for indoor air quality. Reference-only: no product recommendations.

Quick answer

Compliance is typically verified via certifications or testing programs such as FloorScore.

What it is How it's reported Why it matters FAQ

What it is

VOCs are chemicals that can off-gas; compliance ensures emissions stay below limits.

How it's reported

Specs may list certification names, versions, and certificate numbers.

Why it matters

VOC compliance is important for indoor air quality but does not indicate durability.

FAQ

Is VOC compliance the same as zero VOC? ⌄

No. It means emissions are below program thresholds, not zero.

Do adhesives need VOC compliance too? ⌄

Often yes, especially for glue-down installations.

Does finish type affect VOCs? ⌄

Yes. Some finishes and adhesives emit more than others.
