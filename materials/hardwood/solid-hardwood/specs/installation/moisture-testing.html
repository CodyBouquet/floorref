Solid Hardwood Moisture Testing, Explained What it means • How it's reported • Why it matters

Moisture testing ensures the subfloor and flooring are within acceptable ranges before installation. Reference-only: no product recommendations.

Quick answer

Even engineered hardwood can be damaged by excess moisture or improper acclimation.

What it is How it's reported Why it matters FAQ

What it is

Tests include concrete RH probes, calcium chloride kits, or wood moisture meters.

How it's reported

Specs list acceptable moisture levels and required test methods.

Why it matters

Skipping moisture testing can void warranties and cause cupping or adhesive failure.

FAQ

Is moisture testing required for floating floors? ⌄

Yes, especially over concrete or basements.

What if moisture levels are high? ⌄

Use mitigation systems or delay installation until conditions improve.

Do different products have different limits? ⌄

Yes. Always follow the manufacturer’s specifications.
