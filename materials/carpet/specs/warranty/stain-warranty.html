Quick answer

A stain warranty promises that certain stains can be removed with proper cleaning. Coverage depends on definitions, exclusions, and maintenance requirements.

What it is How it's reported Why it matters FAQ

What it is

Stain warranties specify which substances count as “stains,” the time limits for reporting, and the cleaning steps required to maintain coverage.

How it's reported

Warranty documents list the term length, covered stain categories, and exclusions such as bleach, dyes, or pet-related issues.

Why it matters

Understanding stain warranty terms helps avoid surprises when filing a claim. The warranty does not mean all stains are covered or removable.

FAQ

Does a lifetime stain warranty mean no stains? ⌄

No. It means coverage lasts while you own the carpet, but only for defined stains and with proper maintenance.

Are pet stains usually covered? ⌄

Often not. Many warranties exclude pet-related staining or odors.

Do stain warranties require professional cleaning? ⌄

Many do. Check the warranty for required cleaning intervals or documentation.
