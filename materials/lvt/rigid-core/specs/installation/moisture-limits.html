Spec Sheet Checklist

  • Identify the required moisture test method (ASTM F2170, F1869, etc.).
  • Check the maximum RH percentage or MVER value.
  • Confirm slab pH limits and required primers or barriers.
  • Document test locations and results for warranty purposes.
  • Allow appropriate drying or mitigation time before install.

Common Pitfalls

  • Skipping moisture testing because the slab looks dry.
  • Using a different test method than specified.
  • Assuming floating floors ignore moisture limits.

FAQ

Are moisture limits different for glue-down vs rigid core? ⌄

Yes. Glue-down systems often have tighter limits because adhesive performance depends on moisture control.

What if the slab exceeds the limit? ⌄

Most manufacturers require mitigation systems or extended drying before installation.
