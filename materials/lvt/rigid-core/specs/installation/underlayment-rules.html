Spec Sheet Checklist

  • Determine whether additional underlayment is allowed or prohibited.
  • Check maximum underlayment thickness and density.
  • Review sound ratings tied to specific underlayment types.
  • Confirm moisture barrier requirements.
  • Match underlayment approvals to warranty terms.

Common Pitfalls

  • Adding a second pad on products with attached underlayment.
  • Using underlayment not approved by the manufacturer.
  • Assuming thicker pads always improve acoustics.

FAQ

Can I use any foam underlayment with rigid core? ⌄

No. Many products require specific pads to protect the locking system and meet acoustic targets.

Do underlayment rules apply to glue-down floors? ⌄

Glue-down systems typically do not use underlayment, but check manufacturer guidance.
