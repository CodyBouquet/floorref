Quick Explainers

What it is

Weight per carton lists the total weight of a box of LVT, often used for freight planning and jobsite handling.

Why it matters for LVT

Heavy cartons affect labor planning, storage limits, and shipping costs.

Related specs to compare

Use weight per carton alongside carton coverage and plank size for accurate logistics planning.

Spec Sheet Checklist

  • Confirm weight units (lb or kg).
  • Check the number of planks or tiles per carton.
  • Review pallet weight and stacking limits if available.
  • Plan for lifting or equipment needs on large jobs.
  • Coordinate with delivery and storage constraints.

Common Pitfalls

  • Assuming weight per carton is the same across collections.
  • Ignoring weight limits for elevators or storage racks.
  • Mixing metric and imperial weights in estimates.

FAQ

Does heavier carton mean better quality? ⌄

Not necessarily. Weight depends on core density, size, and packaging.

Is carton weight important for residential jobs? ⌄

Yes, especially for delivery access and installation labor planning.
