Spec Sheet Checklist

  • Confirm warranty duration and whether it is prorated.
  • Check the definition of “wear-through” in the warranty.
  • Review maintenance and cleaning requirements.
  • Confirm coverage for residential vs commercial use.
  • Document installation conditions for warranty eligibility.

Common Pitfalls

  • Assuming wear warranty covers scratches or dents.
  • Ignoring maintenance requirements that can void coverage.
  • Treating wear warranty as a performance rating.

FAQ

Does wear warranty cover scratches? ⌄

Usually no. Wear warranties focus on wear-through, not surface scratches.

Is wear warranty tied to wear layer thickness? ⌄

Often related, but the warranty terms define coverage more precisely.
