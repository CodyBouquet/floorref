Spec Sheet Checklist

  • Confirm the duration and whether it is pro-rated.
  • Check the defined commercial use class or application type.
  • Review maintenance requirements tied to coverage.
  • Look for exclusions related to moisture, rolling loads, or sunlight.
  • Verify if adhesive/system warranties are separate.

Common Pitfalls

  • Assuming commercial warranty means unlimited use cases.
  • Missing maintenance documentation requirements.
  • Overlooking exclusions for certain traffic or cleaning methods.

FAQ

Does commercial warranty cover wear from heavy equipment? ⌄

Not necessarily. Many warranties exclude damage from rolling loads or equipment outside specified limits.

Is commercial warranty the same as traffic class? ⌄

No. Traffic class is a rating system; warranty is a coverage term that may reference the class.
