Spec Sheet Details to Look For

Installation details are the guardrails that keep a floor within warranty coverage.

  • Rolling requirements are typically tied to adhesive type and may specify a two-pass schedule within a set time window.
  • Confirm the unit of measure and whether the value is listed as nominal, minimum, or tested result.
  • Look for the test standard or method cited; results are only comparable when the method matches.
  • Check whether the value applies to the full assembly (plank + underlayment + subfloor) or a single layer.

Installation Checklist

  • Verify required roller weight (often 75–100 lb).
  • Check the time window after placement for rolling.
  • Confirm whether a second rolling pass is required.
  • Ensure the adhesive is within open time limits.
  • Avoid rolling before seams are properly set.

Common Pitfalls

  • Using the wrong roller weight or skipping a second pass.
  • Rolling too early, causing adhesive squeeze-out.
  • Rolling after the adhesive has skinned over.

FAQ

Is rolling required for all glue-down LVT? ⌄

Most systems require rolling, but check the specific adhesive instructions.

Can a hand roller replace a heavy roller? ⌄

Only for small areas or edge work; full floors typically require the specified roller weight.
