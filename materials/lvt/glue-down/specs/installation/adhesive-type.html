  • Check approved substrates and primer requirements.

  • Verify spread rate and trowel notch size for coverage.

Common Pitfalls

  • Assuming any vinyl adhesive is interchangeable across products.
  • Ignoring temperature/humidity ranges that change open time.
  • Skipping manufacturer-approved adhesive lists tied to warranty.

FAQ

Is pressure-sensitive adhesive the same as wet-set? ⌄

No. Pressure-sensitive adhesives allow placement after they tack up, while wet-set adhesives require placement into a wet film and usually have different open times and rolling requirements.

Does adhesive type affect warranty coverage? ⌄

Yes. Many LVT warranties require specific adhesives or systems; using unapproved types can void coverage.
