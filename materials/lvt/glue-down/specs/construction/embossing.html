What it is

Why it matters for LVT

Texture impacts traction, gloss, and how realistic the floor appears, especially in long plank visuals.

Spec Sheet Checklist

  • Check whether embossing is standard or embossed-in-register (EIR).
  • Review texture depth and gloss level if listed.
  • Confirm slip resistance or COF for safety requirements.
  • Look at cleaning and maintenance guidance for textured surfaces.
  • Match embossing with pattern repeat for visual consistency.

Common Pitfalls

  • Assuming deeper embossing always improves slip resistance.
  • Ignoring cleaning requirements in heavily textured surfaces.
  • Expecting EIR on every SKU in a collection.

FAQ

Does embossing affect durability? ⌄

It mainly affects texture and appearance; durability is more tied to wear layer and coatings.

Is EIR worth specifying? ⌄

EIR can improve realism, but it may not change performance specs like wear resistance.
